About Me

I am a instructional designer with a strong foundation in learning theory, instructional design models, and learner-centered design. I specialize in creating clear, engaging, and effective learning experiences that help learners achieve measurable outcomes.

My approach combines structured instructional design with practical problem-solving. I enjoy analyzing learning needs, designing meaningful learning activities, and developing content that is accessible, well-organized, and aligned with learning objectives.

I am particularly interested in designing online learning, corporate training, and educational content that is intuitive, inclusive, and results-driven.
